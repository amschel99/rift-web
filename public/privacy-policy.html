Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Rift Payment Solutions ("Rift," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our digital payment platform and related services (the "Services").

Rift is a neo-bank platform that enables users to:

  • Accept USDC and M-Pesa payments
  • Convert cryptocurrency to Kenyan Shillings (KES) and other fiat currencies
  • Send and receive digital payments
  • Access prediction markets and trading services
  • Manage digital wallets and cryptocurrency assets

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including:

Account Registration:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Username and password
  • Profile photo (optional)

Identity Verification (KYC):

  • Government-issued identification documents
  • Biometric data (selfie photos, facial recognition data)
  • Proof of address documents
  • Nationality and country of residence
  • Employment information (if required)

Financial Information:

  • Bank account details for withdrawals
  • M-Pesa account information
  • Transaction history and payment preferences
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Investment preferences and risk tolerance

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device and Technical Information:

  • IP address and geolocation data
  • Device type, operating system, and browser information
  • Mobile device identifiers
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Network connection information

Transaction Data:

  • Payment amounts and currencies
  • Transaction timestamps and descriptions
  • Merchant and recipient information
  • Blockchain transaction hashes
  • Fee information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Processing payments and cryptocurrency transactions
  • Converting between cryptocurrencies and fiat currencies
  • Facilitating M-Pesa and traditional banking integrations
  • Maintaining and managing your digital wallet
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance

2.2 Legal and Compliance

  • Verifying your identity (KYC/AML compliance)
  • Preventing fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities
  • Complying with financial regulations and reporting requirements
  • Responding to legal requests and law enforcement inquiries
  • Tax reporting and documentation

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers who help us operate our Services:

  • Payment Processors: Safaricom (M-Pesa), banking partners, cryptocurrency exchanges
  • Identity Verification: Smile ID for KYC compliance
  • Cloud Services: Data hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics: Usage tracking and performance monitoring services
  • Communication: Email and SMS service providers
Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Secure socket layer (SSL) technology for web communications
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Segregated storage of cryptocurrency private keys
