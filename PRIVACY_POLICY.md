# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 12, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Rift Payment Solutions ("Rift," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our digital payment platform and related services (the "Services").

Rift is a neo-bank platform that enables users to:

- Accept USDC and M-Pesa payments
- Convert cryptocurrency to Kenyan Shillings (KES) and other fiat currencies
- Send and receive digital payments
- Access prediction markets and trading services
- Manage digital wallets and cryptocurrency assets

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including:

**Account Registration:**

- Full name and contact information

**Identity Verification (KYC):**

- Government-issued identification documents

- Employment information (if required)

**Financial Information:**

- Bank account details for withdrawals
- M-Pesa account information

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device and Technical Information:**

- IP address and geolocation data

**Transaction Data:**

**Analytics and Usage Data:**

- Feature usage statistics
