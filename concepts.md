# Rift Marketing Video Concepts

---

## Concept 1: "No Borders"

**Duration:** 45–60 seconds
**Tone:** Bold, cinematic, empowering
**Music:** Afrobeats instrumental with a slow build — starts minimal, drops hard at the "send" moment

---

### Story

A young professional in Nairobi needs to send money to her brother in Lagos. She opens her phone. No bank queues. No Western Union. No waiting days. Just Rift.

---

### Shot List / Scenes

| # | Scene | Visual | Duration | Notes |
|---|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | **Open on a map** | Animated dark map of Africa, glowing dots on Nairobi, Lagos, Kampala, Dar es Salaam, Kinshasa, Lilongwe. Lines pulse between them. | 3s | Set the stage — this is continental |
| 2 | **The problem** | Quick cuts: long bank queues, Western Union storefront, someone frustrated staring at a "3–5 business days" screen, fees showing $15 on a $100 send | 5s | Black & white or desaturated — the old world |
| 3 | **Color drops in** | A phone screen lights up. Rift logo. Color floods the frame. | 2s | Transition moment — everything shifts |
| 4 | **The wallet** | Close-up of the Rift home screen — balance showing, multiple currencies. Thumb taps "Wallet" | 3s | Screen recording with clean phone mockup |
| 5 | **Send money** | User taps Send Money > Nigeria > enters amount (5,000 NGN) > selects GTBank > types account number | 5s | Sped-up screen recording, smooth and confident |
| 6 | **Verification** | OTP boxes fill in — 4 digits, auto-submits | 2s | Satisfying animation — each box fills with a subtle pop |
| 7 | **The drop** | "Payment sent" confirmation. Music drops. Camera pulls back to reveal the map — a glowing line shoots from Nairobi to Lagos | 4s | This is the hero shot. The line should feel electric, like energy traveling |
| 8 | **Lagos receives** | Split screen: sender smiling in Nairobi / brother in Lagos gets a bank alert on his phone, smiles | 4s | Warm, real, human |
| 9 | **Montage — all corridors** | Quick cuts of the map lines firing: Kenya > Uganda, Tanzania > Congo, Brazil glowing on a world map. Each line accompanied by a currency symbol flying across (KSh, NGN, USh, TSh, FC, R$) | 6s | Fast, energetic, shows scale |
| 10 | **Crypto layer** | Subtle zoom into the phone — shows USDC/USDT tokens, the conversion happening under the hood. Text overlay: "Powered by stablecoins. Settled in seconds." | 4s | Don't over-explain crypto — just hint at the engine |
| 11 | **Features flash** | Quick animated cards sliding in: "Withdraw to M-Pesa" / "Send to any bank" / "No hidden fees" / "Works on any phone" | 4s | Clean motion graphics, Rift brand colors |
| 12 | **End card** | Rift logo centered. Tagline fades in below. App store badges. | 4s | Clean, confident, minimal |

---

### Images to Create

1. **Dark Africa map with glowing city nodes** — Nairobi, Lagos, Kampala, Dar es Salaam, Kinshasa, Lilongwe, Sao Paulo all as bright dots connected by faint grid lines. Dark navy/black background, accent-colored dots (use Rift brand green/teal).

2. **Phone mockup — Rift home screen** — Clean iPhone/Android frame showing the actual Rift balance screen with "1,269.12 KES" balance, the currency selector, and action buttons. Floating slightly with a soft shadow.

3. **Split-screen sender/receiver** — Left half: warm-toned photo of a young woman on her phone in a modern Nairobi setting (coffee shop, co-working space). Right half: young man in Lagos checking his phone with a smile. Divided by a glowing Rift-colored line.

4. **Currency symbols constellation** — KSh, NGN, USh, TSh, FC, MK, R$ floating in space like stars, with subtle connecting lines between them. Abstract, could be used as a background or transition frame.

5. **"Old way vs Rift" comparison graphic** — Left: grayscale icons of bank queue, clock showing "3-5 days", fee tag showing "$15". Right: colored Rift logo, lightning bolt, fee tag showing "$0.15". Clean infographic style.

6. **Stablecoin engine visual** — Abstract illustration showing USDC/USDT coins flowing through a pipeline that converts to local currency symbols on the other end. Technical but beautiful — like a cross-section of the infrastructure.

7. **End card** — Rift logo large and centered on dark background. Tagline below. Subtle particle effects or gentle glow around the logo.

---

### Tagline Options

- "Send money. Skip borders."
- "Your money. No borders."
- "Africa's wallet. No boundaries."
- "Move money like it's a message."

---

### Music Direction

- Genre: Afrobeats instrumental / Afro-electronic
- Starts soft — ambient pads, maybe a kalimba or soft keys
- Builds with light percussion during the "problem" scenes
- Full drop with drums + bass when the payment sends (scene 7)
- Stays energetic through the montage
- Pulls back to just the melody for the end card
- Reference vibe: Burna Boy instrumentals meets tech-product launch energy

---

### Color Palette

- Primary: Rift brand teal/green (accent-primary)
- Background: Deep navy / near-black (#0a0e1a)
- Accents: White text, soft glows in teal
- "Old world" scenes: Desaturated, grey-blue tint
- "Rift world" scenes: Full color, warm undertones for people, cool teal for UI

- Scene 7: "Sent. In seconds."
